About Me

Athens born and Georgia raised, I am proud to call the South my home. I am a multimedia designer and creator with enthusiasm for the arts and refining visual presentation. My passions stem from creativity and versatile strengths that fuel my drive for success. My interests can be traced back to my eye for detail. In the recent past, I have strived to grow my connections and opportunities in order to improve my reputation and skill set. I hope to continue my journey in the business and entrepreneurial worlds and am always looking for a challenge.

Artistic endeavors have been a cornerstone of my progression and passions for most of my life. I have been blessed to be able to develop my talents over the years and be a part of some cool projects in the past.

Hand-in-hand with my love for visual arts is my enthusiasm for video shooting, editing, and all around production. I hope to expand my experience in video production more in the future.
